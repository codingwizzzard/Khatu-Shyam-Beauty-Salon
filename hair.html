Haircut & Hairstyling

What We Offer

For Hair, we go beyond styling and routine services. Our specialists with over 5 years of experience curate every hair ritual with precision—starting with an AI-powered hair consultation, a first in Borivali. This technology maps your scalp and hair health in real time, helping us understand what your hair truly needs.

Whether it’s a global color, root touch-up, haircut, or our highly loved Korean Scalp Treatment, every service is personalized to give you healthier, shinier, and more manageable hair.
